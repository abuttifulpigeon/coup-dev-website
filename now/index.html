What's happening now?

Last updated 2025-02-11

Alright, what's happening?

I just started the final semester of my legally required educational career. In a little over three months I'll be walking across that stage... for precisely 3 seconds, that is. All jokes aside this is and forever will be one of the largest stepping stones in my life, and I'm not just saying that. My entire life I've always felt like the "kid", either they were older than me and thus superior, or they were just stupid kids. But now, that's changed. I'm currently the oldest in my school and it's definitely strange. I'm not gonna get all hubby dubby and whatnot, this is a now page not a memoir, but, if you are curious to learn, more do read my about page.
